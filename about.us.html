About SSM Modern Bakery

COMPANY PROFILE

SSM Modern Bakery opened in 2019 as a bakery run by three good friends. Our bakery was named in conjunction with our names, namely Syakira, Suziatty and also Maziah, which is became SSM. Our product range has diversified and expanded to meet the needs of a growing customer base. Our store is more focused on selling and ordering cakes by producing various types and is in high demand for wedding and birthday orders. In the store we provide Cash on delivery service to the customer's home for cake orders and also self pickup to our store according to the customer's convenience. Our great team consisting of trained workers and our well-known group of vehicles ensures all items are produced and distributed fresh to our customers.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to provide a growing portfolio of high quality and specialized products in the marketplace, while maintaining outstanding customer satisfaction in every interaction
